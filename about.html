THE WINTER COMMITMENT

Winter in Jammu & Kashmir isn't just a season; it's a structural challenge. When the temperature drops below zero, standard plumbing fails. We have invested in high-end thermal thawing machinery and insulated piping solutions to ensure that our customers never have to face a dry tap in the middle of January. Our "Winter Shield" protocols are active from November to March, prioritizing elderly households and schools.
